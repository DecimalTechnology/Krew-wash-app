## Quick Reference

### Data Collected
- Personal Information: Name, Email, Phone, Profile Photo
- Location: Precise and approximate location
- Vehicle Information: Type, Model, Number, Color, Parking
- Booking Information: Packages, Dates, Payment
- Device Information: Device ID, Push tokens
- Usage Data: Analytics, app interactions

### Third-Party Services
- **Firebase (Google)**: Authentication, Database, Analytics, Push Notifications
- **Google Services**: Sign-In, Maps
- **Telr**: Payment Processing
- **Socket.IO**: Real-time Communication

### Permissions Required
- **Location**: Fine and Coarse (Optional)
- **Camera**: For profile photos (Optional)
- **Storage**: For image selection (Optional)

### Data Sharing
- Service Providers/Cleaners: For service delivery
- Firebase: For app functionality
- Payment Gateway: For payment processing
- Backend API: For service management
