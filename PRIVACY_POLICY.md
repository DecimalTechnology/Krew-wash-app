## 1. Introduction

Welcome to Krew Car Wash ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

By using our App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following personal information when you register and use our App:

- **Name**: Your full name for account identification and service personalization
- **Email Address**: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
- **Phone Number**: Required for account verification via SMS OTP and service communication
- **Profile Photo**: Optional profile picture uploaded by you
- **Building Information**: Building ID and apartment number for service delivery
- **Authentication Method**: Information about how you verified your account (Google Sign-In, Email, Phone)

### 2.2 Vehicle Information

To provide car wash services, we collect:

- **Vehicle Type**: Type of vehicle (e.g., car, SUV, truck)
- **Vehicle Model**: Specific model of your vehicle
- **Vehicle Number**: License plate or registration number
- **Vehicle Color**: Color of your vehicle
- **Parking Number**: Parking spot number for service location identification

### 2.3 Booking and Service Information

When you make a booking, we collect:

- **Package Selection**: Selected car wash package details
- **Add-On Services**: Additional services you choose
- **Service Dates**: Selected dates and times for service delivery
- **Service Location**: Your building and apartment information
- **Payment Information**: Transaction amounts, payment status, and booking IDs
- **Service History**: Records of completed, cancelled, and pending bookings

### 2.4 Location Information

With your permission, we collect:

- **Precise Location**: Fine and coarse location data to:
  - Display your location on Google Maps
  - Help service providers locate your vehicle
  - Provide location-based service recommendations

Location data is collected only when you grant permission and is used solely for service delivery purposes.

### 2.5 Device and Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, and version
- **App Usage Data**: Features used, interaction patterns, and app performance
- **Firebase Analytics Data**: Aggregated usage statistics and crash reports
- **Push Notification Tokens**: Device tokens for sending notifications

### 2.6 Communication Data

If you use our in-app chat or messaging features:

- **Chat Messages**: Messages exchanged between you and service providers
- **Communication Preferences**: Your notification and communication settings

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the following circumstances:

  - Google Sign-In: Social authentication

- **Telr Payment Gateway**: Payment processing services

  - Service coordination

### 4.3 Legal Requirements

- **Legal Process**: Court orders, subpoenas, or other legal processes

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Data Storage

Your data is stored in:

- **Local Device Storage**: Encrypted secure storage on your device for:
  - Authentication tokens
  - User profile data
  - Temporary booking information

- **Firebase Cloud Firestore**: Cloud database for:
  - User accounts
  - Booking records
  - Service history

- **Backend API Server**: Our hosted server for:
  - User management
  - Booking management
  - Service coordination

### 5.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Data encryption in transit (HTTPS) and at rest
- **Secure Storage**: Use of Flutter Secure Storage with encrypted shared preferences
- **Authentication**: Secure token-based authentication
- **Access Controls**: Limited access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Updates**: Security updates and patches to protect against vulnerabilities

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
